Frequently Asked Questions

We proudly serve the SGV (San Gabriel Valley) and LA (Los Angeles) areas. Our mobile detailing service comes to you, wherever you are in these regions.

The duration of our detailing service can vary depending on the package you choose and the condition of your vehicle. A basic exterior wash might take about an hour, while a full interior and exterior detailing could take 3-5 hours. We'll provide you with a time estimate when you book your appointment.

To prepare for your appointment, please ensure that your vehicle is accessible and that we have access to water and electricity if needed. Remove any personal items from your car that you don't want to be moved or cleaned around. If you're getting interior detailing, it's helpful to remove large items to allow our team better access to all areas of your vehicle.

Yes, we are fully self-sufficient with our own water and power supply. However, access to your water and electricity can help us provide an even more thorough service.

We accept all major credit cards, cash, and digital payments including Venmo, Zelle, and Cash App for your convenience.

For optimal results, we recommend getting your car detailed every 3-4 months. However, this can vary based on your driving conditions, climate, and how you use your vehicle.
